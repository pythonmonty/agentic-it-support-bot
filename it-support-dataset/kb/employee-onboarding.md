# Employee Onboarding

**Article ID:** KB-023

## Summary
Provisioning identity, access, and devices for new joiners. Provisioning can lag the start date.

## Symptoms
- New joiner cannot log in on day one
- Some apps provisioned, others missing
- Laptop not yet enrolled

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Confirm the HR record and start date triggered provisioning.
2. Check which role-based entitlements have synced vs pending.
3. Verify device enrollment status.

## Resolution Steps
1. Trigger or accelerate provisioning for missing entitlements.
2. Enroll the device and apply compliance policies.
3. Validate access to day-one applications.

## Escalation Guidance
Provisioning stuck beyond SLA -> Identity and Access Management.

## Related Systems
- Identity Governance Platform
- Azure AD
- Endpoint Management
