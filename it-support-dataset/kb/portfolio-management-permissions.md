# Portfolio Management Permissions

**Article ID:** KB-015

## Summary
Permissions that control visibility of portfolios and books in the Portfolio Management System.

## Symptoms
- Portfolio dashboard loads but is empty
- Can see some books but not others
- "No data for your selection"
- Lost portfolio view after transfer

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Check the portfolio access role is assigned.
2. Confirm book/desk-level scoping matches the user's coverage.
3. Verify provisioning sync completed after any role change.

## Resolution Steps
1. Assign the missing portfolio access role and correct book scoping.
2. Refresh permissions and re-open the dashboard.
3. Validate the expected portfolios are visible.

## Escalation Guidance
Role assigned but data still empty -> Portfolio Systems team.

## Related Systems
- Portfolio Management System
- Identity Governance Platform
