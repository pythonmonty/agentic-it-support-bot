# Client Reporting Portal Access

**Article ID:** KB-017

## Summary
Permissions for generating and viewing client reports.

## Symptoms
- Cannot generate or download client reports
- Report list is empty
- "Permission denied" when exporting
- New advisor cannot see assigned clients

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Confirm the client reporting permission/role is assigned.
2. Check client-book scoping for the advisor.
3. Verify provisioning completed after onboarding/role change.

## Resolution Steps
1. Assign client reporting permissions with correct scoping.
2. Refresh permissions and reload the portal.
3. Validate report generation.

## Escalation Guidance
Permissions correct but exports fail -> Reporting Systems team.

## Related Systems
- Client Reporting Portal
- Identity Governance Platform
