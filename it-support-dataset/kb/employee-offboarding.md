# Employee Offboarding

**Article ID:** KB-024

## Summary
Disabling access for leavers and handling residual/shared access.

## Symptoms
- Leaver still appears active
- Shared mailbox/app access lingered after departure
- Reactivation needed for a rehire

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Confirm the HR termination event processed.
2. Check for orphaned group memberships or shared access.
3. For rehires, verify a fresh provisioning cycle.

## Resolution Steps
1. Disable the account and remove residual access.
2. For inactive-but-needed accounts, follow reactivation with approval.
3. Validate the leaver has no remaining access.

## Escalation Guidance
Residual privileged access -> Security review.

## Related Systems
- Identity Governance Platform
- Azure AD
